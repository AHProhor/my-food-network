About Me

I am Gordon James Ramsay a British chef, restaurateur, television personality and writer. I was was born in Johnstone, Scotland, and raised in Stratford-upon-Avon, England. I founded my global restaurant group, Gordon Ramsay Restaurants, in 1997. It has been awarded 16 Michelin stars in total and currently holds a total of seven. After rising to fame on the British television miniseries Boiling Point in 1999, I had become one of the best-known and most influential chefs in the UK by 2004.
